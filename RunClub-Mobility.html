Session 1
Pre-Run Dynamic Warmup
GYMGEEKRun Club Session 1 · Pre-Run Dynamic Warmup
Block A · Hip Openers
A1
Leg Swings (Forward & Back)
Hold a wall or post for balance. Swing freely — let momentum do the work, don't force range.
3 sets 15 each leg
A2
Leg Swings (Side to Side)
Face the wall, swing across the body. Keep the standing leg soft, not locked.
3 sets 15 each leg
A3
Hip Circles
Stand on one leg, draw big slow circles with the raised knee. Full range each rep.
3 sets 10 each direction
Block B · Ankle & Calf Prep
B1
Ankle Circles
Seated or standing. Slow and controlled — runners often skip this and pay for it later.
3 sets 10 each direction
B2
Calf Raises (Single Leg)
Use a step for extra range if available. Control the lowering phase — 2 seconds down.
3 sets 12 each leg
B3
Heel-to-Toe Walks
Walk 10m on heels, 10m on toes. Activates the tibialis anterior — great injury prevention.
3 sets 10m each
Block C · Glute & Core Activation
C1
Glute Bridges
Squeeze at the top for 1 second. Feet hip-width, drive through heels. Wake the glutes up before they're needed.
3 sets 15 reps
C2
Dead Bugs
Press your lower back into the floor throughout. Slow, deliberate — this is about control not speed.
3 sets 8 each side
C3
High Knees (marching tempo)
Drive the arms in opposition. Build to a jog pace over the set — this is your final gear-up before you head out.
3 sets 30 sec

Session 2
Post-Run Static Cool Down
GYMGEEKRun Club Session 2 · Post-Run Static Cool Down
Block A · Lower Body — Hold Each Side
A1
Standing Quad Stretch
Hold ankle behind you, keep knees together. Stand tall — don't lean forward at the hip.
3 sets 45 sec each
A2
Standing Calf Stretch
Back foot flat on the floor, front knee bent over toes. Then bend back knee slightly for the soleus — do both positions.
3 sets 40 sec each
A3
Half Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch
Tuck the pelvis under slightly — you should feel this deep in the front of the back hip, not in the knee.
3 sets 45 sec each
A4
Seated Hamstring Stretch
Sit tall, hinge from the hips — don't round the back to reach further. Think chest toward shin, not forehead toward knee.
3 sets 45 sec each
Block B · Upper Body & Back
B1
Cross-Body Shoulder Stretch
Running tightens the shoulders more than people realise — especially after long efforts. Hold gently, breathe out to deepen.
3 sets 30 sec each
B2
Cat-Cow
Slow and rhythmic. Breathe in on cow (arch), breathe out on cat (round). 5 slow breaths per rep.
3 sets 10 reps
B3
Child's Pose
Arms extended or resting alongside the body — whichever feels more comfortable. Stay here for the full time. You earned it.
3 sets 60 sec

Session 3
Full Runner's Mobility Session
GYMGEEKRun Club Session 3 · Full Runner's Mobility — Rest Day
Block A · Hip Complex
A1
90/90 Hip Stretch
Sit with both knees at 90 degrees. Lean into the front shin to feel the external rotator of the back hip. Switch sides.
3 sets 60 sec each
A2
Pigeon Pose
From a lunge, bring the front shin across the mat. Ease into it — this is not a competition. Breathe through the discomfort.
3 sets 60 sec each
A3
Deep Lunge with Rotation
Step into a long lunge, drop the back knee down. Place hand inside the front foot and rotate the chest open toward the ceiling.
3 sets 8 each side
Block B · Posterior Chain
B1
Supine Hamstring Stretch (Band or Strap)
Lie on your back, loop a towel or band around the foot. Pull gently — stop when you feel a mild pull, not a sharp stretch.
3 sets 45 sec each
B2
Figure-4 Glute Stretch
Lying on your back, cross one ankle over the opposite knee and draw both legs toward your chest. The crossed leg's glute and piriformis should light up.
3 sets 50 sec each
B3
Downward Dog Calf Walk-Out
In downward dog, slowly pedal the heels one at a time — pressing each down toward the floor for 2 seconds. This targets gastrocnemius and soleus together.
3 sets 10 each side
Block C · Thoracic Spine & Ankle
C1
Thoracic Rotation (Seated or Quadruped)
Hand behind head, rotate the elbow toward the ceiling. Runners who neglect thoracic rotation compensate through the lower back. Don't be that runner.
3 sets 10 each side
C2
Ankle Dorsiflexion Lunge
Stand 10cm from a wall, drive the knee forward to touch it. Keep the heel flat. Move the foot back until the heel just lifts — that's your mobility edge. Work in that range.
3 sets 15 each ankle
C3
Supine Spinal Twist
Finish here. Lie on your back, drop both knees to one side, arms out wide. Take 5 slow breaths. Switch. This is your sign-off — don't rush it.
3 sets 5 breaths each
